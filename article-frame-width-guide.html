Pick the Right Frame Width

Avoid the most common fit mistake by using temple-to-temple proportion checks.

3 min readFit GuideQuick Tips

Simple Rule

Frame width should be close to your face width. If rims extend far beyond temples, size down.

Signs of Wrong Width

  • Too wide: slides down and looks oversized.
  • Too narrow: leaves marks and feels tight.

Try Before Finalizing

Turn your head and smile. Good width stays balanced without pressure points.
