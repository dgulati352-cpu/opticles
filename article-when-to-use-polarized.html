When to Use Polarized Lenses

Know the best scenarios for polarization so you get comfort exactly when you need it.

4 min readLens GuidePractical Tips

Use for High Glare Conditions

Polarized lenses are excellent for roads, beaches, lakes, and bright city reflections.

When Not Necessary

For low-light or indoor use, polarization may reduce brightness too much and offers less value.

Combine With UV400

Polarization reduces glare, but UV400 gives sun protection. The best setup combines both.
