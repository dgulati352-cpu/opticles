Best Frames by Face Shape

Match shape contrast and frame width to improve overall balance instantly.

6 min readFace FitBeginner

Contrast Creates Balance

Round faces look sharper with angular lines. Strong jawlines look softer with curved rims.

Keep Scale Proportional

Small faces should avoid oversized heavy rims. Broader faces need enough frame width to avoid pinching.

Comfort Check

Final fit should sit straight, avoid nose pressure, and stay stable while moving.
