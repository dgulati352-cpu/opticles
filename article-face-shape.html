How To Pick Frames For Your Face Shape

Use simple shape matching rules to choose frames that naturally balance your features.

6 min read Beginner friendly Style Guide

Start With Face Proportion

Your face shape is a guide, not a strict rulebook. Check forehead width, cheek width, and jaw angle to understand whether your features are soft, sharp, or balanced.

Quick Matching Rules

  • Round face: choose angular frames like square and rectangular shapes.
  • Square face: choose curved lines such as round or oval frames.
  • Oval face: most frame types work, focus on frame size balance.
  • Heart face: try thinner lower rims and softer edges.

Check Width Before Style

The frame front should align closely with your face width. Too wide can slip; too narrow can pinch. Comfort always wins over trends.

Final Try-On Checklist

Turn your head, smile, and look down. If the frame stays stable without pressure marks, the fit is right.
