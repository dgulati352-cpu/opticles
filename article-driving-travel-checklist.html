Driving and Travel Shade Checklist

Essential lens and frame checks before your next road trip or outdoor journey.

4 min readTravelChecklist

Lens Essentials

  • UV400 protection for daylight exposure.
  • Polarized lens to cut windshield glare.
  • Neutral tint for accurate traffic signal visibility.

Frame Essentials

Choose stable temples, no-slip nose pads, and lightweight construction for long wear comfort.

Carry Kit

Keep a hard case, microfiber cloth, and backup pair in your travel bag.
