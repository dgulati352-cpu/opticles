Lens Coatings 101

A quick guide to anti-glare, anti-scratch, blue-cut, and water-repellent lens layers.

4 min readLens CareBeginner

Why Coatings Matter

Coatings improve comfort, durability, and visibility. They are often more important than frame design for daily use.

Most Useful Coatings

  • Anti-glare reduces reflections from screens and headlights.
  • Hard coat improves scratch resistance.
  • Hydrophobic coat repels water and smudges.
  • Blue-cut filter helps with long screen sessions.

Best Combo

For daily wear, combine anti-glare + scratch resistant + UV protection.
