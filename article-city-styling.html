City Styling: Shades For Work, Travel, Night

Build a 3-look rotation so one eyewear set works from meetings to weekends.

7 min read Styling Tips Daily Wear

Look 1: Office Smart

Choose neutral frame colors like matte black, gunmetal, or tortoise. Keep lines clean and lens tint subtle for professional settings.

Look 2: Travel Utility

For commute and trips, prioritize comfort and glare control. Polarized wrap or aviator designs work well for long outdoor exposure.

Look 3: Evening Social

Pick expressive shapes like round retro or geometric frames with gradient tints. They add personality without feeling loud.

One Collection Strategy

  • Choose a base frame for daily wear.
  • Add one statement frame for weekends.
  • Use one sports-oriented pair for travel and harsh light.
